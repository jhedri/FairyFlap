Fairy Flap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Fairy Flap is developed by Jeffrey Hedrick. This policy describes how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

Fairy Flap does not require an account and does not collect personal information. Game scores (last score and high score) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage.

Third-Party Services

Fairy Flap does not use third-party analytics or advertising services.

Children's Privacy

Fairy Flap is designed to be appropriate for kids. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
